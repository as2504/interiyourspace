Philosophy of Light and Space

"Design is not just what it looks like; it's how it makes you feel."

The Serene Residence project was born from a desire to create a sanctuary that breathes. Located in the heart of the city, this penthouse required a complete spatial reimagining to optimize the flow of natural light and capitalize on panoramic views.

By applying the interiyourspace design philosophy, we focused on the intersection of materiality and illumination. Every surface, from the hand-applied plaster walls to the custom white-oak cabinetry, was selected for its ability to catch and diffuse light throughout the day.
